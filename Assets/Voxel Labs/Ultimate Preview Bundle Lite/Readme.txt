# Bundle Asset User Guide

- When you purchase this bundle pack, you unlock free access to multiple individual assets. The bundle itself does not contain files; instead, it provides free access to every asset included in the pack.

-To access the included assets, follow the steps below:

1. Access to Assets

After purchasing the bundle, all assets included in it will automatically become free for your account. Visit each package listed above, add them to your cart, and checkout for free.

2. How to Download

You can download each asset individually at no cost through the Unity Package Manager.

3. Alternative Access Method

You can also open any included asset’s product page and click “Open in Unity” to download it.
